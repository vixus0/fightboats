Task

You'll be creating a digital version of a certain well-known game.

The game is played with two people. (If you finish early, you can try and create a version where you play against the computer!)

Tips

Work in pairs. You're less likely to get stuck.

Figure out what the different objects in your game are, what they do and what they need to communicate with before you start coding. You can use CRC.

When you start coding, use the rabble timer to make sure you're not hogging the keyboard.
